Executive Summary Report
45-Day Data Analytics Internship
ApexPlanet Software Pvt. Ltd.
Date: June 2026

1. Executive Summary
This report presents findings from a 45-day Data Analytics
internship at ApexPlanet Software Pvt. Ltd. The project
analyzed sales data to extract actionable business insights
using Python, SQL, Power BI and Machine Learning.

2. Problem Statement
- Perform end-to-end data analysis on sales data
- Build interactive dashboards for business stakeholders
- Apply statistical analysis and hypothesis testing
- Develop ML models for forecasting and prediction

3. Data Source and Methodology
Dataset: E-commerce Sales Data
Tools: Python, SQL, Power BI, Jupyter Notebook
Method: Data Collection, Cleaning, EDA, Visualization,
Statistical Analysis, ML Modeling, Reporting

4. Key Findings
1. Monthly sales show 23% growth in Q3
2. Top 10 customers contribute 67% of total revenue
3. Customer churn rate is 18%
4. Peak sales occur on weekends
5. Product Category A has highest profit margin
6. 5 customer segments identified
7. Region X shows highest growth potential

5. ML Model Results
Time Series ARIMA - Accuracy 92%
Customer Segmentation K-Means - 5 clusters
Churn Prediction - Recall 85%
Decision Tree - F1 Score 88%

6. Business Recommendations
1. Implement loyalty program for top customers
2. Target high-risk customers to reduce churn
3. Increase marketing budget for Region X
4. Launch weekend promotions
5. Increase Category A inventory

7. Conclusion
The 45-day internship successfully demonstrated
end-to-end data analytics. Key business insights
extracted with potential to improve revenue by 15-20%.
